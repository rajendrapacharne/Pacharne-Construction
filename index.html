About

About Us

Pacharne Construction

Pacharne Construction is a renowned civil contracting company based in Mawal, Pune. With over two decades of experience in the field, we have been serving our clients with dedication and expertise since our establishment in 2002.

At Pacharne Construction, we specialize in constructing new houses and renovating old buildings, providing comprehensive solutions to meet our clients' diverse needs. Over the years, we have successfully completed numerous projects, including residential homes, commercial complexes, hotels, temples, farmhouses, and more.

Our commitment to excellence, coupled with our team of skilled professionals and use of advanced construction techniques, ensures that every project we undertake is executed with precision and quality craftsmanship. We prioritize client satisfaction and strive to exceed expectations with every project we undertake.

Whether it's building a dream home or renovating a heritage building, Pacharne Construction is dedicated to delivering superior results that stand the test of time. We take pride in our workmanship and look forward to continuing our legacy of excellence in the construction industry.
